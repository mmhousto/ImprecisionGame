Imprecision

Imprecision is a first/third person, action-adventure, rogue-lite set in the Scandinavian Middle Ages. Developed by MM's Gaming Community LLC.

ESRB: T

Controls

  • Move: WASD/Left Stick

  • Look: Mouse/Right Stick

  • Aim: RMB/Left Trigger

  • Pullback/Fire: LMB/Right Trigger

  • Run: Shift/Left Stick Click

  • Jump: Space/A

Imprecision Story

In Imprecision, you play as a lone archer, Örvar, who is training to take down The Black Knights of Bjørn and their kingdom. King Bjørn the Bloodthirsty and The Black Knights have taken over the nation destroying a lot of the land and enslaving towns. If you are not part of the royal kingdom then you are a thrall. You are determined to take back what is yours.
